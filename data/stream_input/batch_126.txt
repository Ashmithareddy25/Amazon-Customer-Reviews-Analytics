There's this weird button and if you push on it, the dark visor pops down. That was extremely cool, I've never had a helmet like that before. And pushing a different part of the button will make it rise. The visor is easy to use, even with bulky motorcycle gloves. On my bike (idle): Music is still crystal clear. Had to probably adjust the knob a little to make it louder. Although I had to adjust the volume so that I could hear the other person over the sound of the wind/traffic, which is generally a pain, considering how the controls are so sensitive.  Riding my bike on the freeway (roughly 70-75mph): Music still gets tricky now. The knob, which is VERY sensitive, adjusts the volume, AT THE TOUCH OF THE WIND. How annoying is that. The person on the other end says that it really sounds like I just have the phone to my ear and that I'm not on a bike or a tunnel, except maybe that I'm yelling a lot, because I cannot hear myself talk.
