I only wish it was written while he was here to see it with his own eyes.... I hope he is able to feel the love... God Bless you Michael!<br /><br />***final review, nut shell... worth the read, educated author, well written, too much repeat repeat, thank you for caring enough to write this book Dr. Moriarty....
