This song is my #1 choice for the next single and definitely my favorite in terms of sheer pop catchiness and melody. Never in my life have I been so moved by the intent behind the meaning of a song. Add this song to the growing list of \\"mass shooting\\" songs (Pearl Jam's \\"Jeremy\\" and Korn's \\"Thoughtless\\" immediately come to mind) and you have the unparalleled Leader of the Pack in my opinion. By this point in the album, if you are not entirely convinced that David Bowie is at the peak of his powers as a genuine artist, then all I can think of to say is...you're not paying attention.
